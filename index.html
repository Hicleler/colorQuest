Combine the colors and get these 4 tiles on your board!

New Game

How to play: Use your finger to move the tiles below the gray line.
When two tiles touch, they merge into one and numbers add up!
If you manage to get all tiles matched on the board at a time, you win.
Note: Number more than 16 will be looped back to count from 1.
